What if a blank screen appears when I access the Units?

Ensure that your computer system meets the Course System Requirements. The Course System Requirements are available on the course homepage under Course Resources.

How do I configure my internet browser to permit pop-up windows?

RECA Education courses occasionally use multiple windows. Pop-up windows are small additional windows that appear on the computer screen. Your internet browser and security software must be set to accept pop-up windows from RECA (reca.ca) in order for you to be able to view them. To configure your internet browser to always accept pop-up windows from RECA:

Internet Explorer

  1. Click the Tools button.
  2. Click Internet Options.
  3. Click the Privacy tab and click Settings under Pop-up Blocker.
  4. Type ‘reca.ca’ in the box under ‘Address of website to allow’.
  5. Click Add, click Close and then click OK to exit Internet Options.

Firefox
  1. Click the Firefox menu.
  2. Click Options.
  3. Click the Content tab.
  4. Click the Exceptions button next to Block Pop-up windows.
  5. Type ‘reca.ca , in the box under ‘Address of website:’.
  6. Click Allow, click Close and then click OK to exit Options.

Safari
  1. In Safari, navigate to http://map.reca.ca/MAP/login/index.php/
  2. Go to the Safari menu and if there is a checkmark, click Block Pop-Up Windows.
  3. Click the safari menu again and ensure that the Block Pop-Up Windows function is disabled (i.e. the checkmark has disappeared)

How do I navigate through the online course?

Information on how to navigate through the course is available in the Course Orientation. To review the Course Orientation, click on the Resources tab in the Unit you are viewing and then click on Course Orientation.

How can I print the course screens?

The course screens are not printer-friendly. The instructional material that appears on screen is available in the eBook for the Unit you are reading. To access the eBook for the Unit you are reading, click on the Resources tab and then click on Unit eBook.

How do I review instructional material that I have already viewed?

To return to the previous screen, click the Previous button at the bottom of the screen you are on. To return to an earlier screen within the same Unit, click on the applicable heading from the Table of Contents on the left side of the screen.

How do I log out of the course when I am finished studying?

Click on the Exit button to return to the course homepage. From the course homepage, you can log out of the course by clicking on Logout next to your name in the top right corner of the screen. You must log out of the course for your progress to be tracked.

Do I need to log out of the course each time I am finished studying?

You must always log out of the course each time you are finished studying in order for your progress to be tracked. If you do not log out of the course, your progress will not be tracked.

How do I return to the place in the course where I left off before logging out?

You must log out of the course in order for your progress to be tracked. If you have logged out correctly, the course will automatically remember where you left off when you logged out and will return you to that point in the course when you log in again. You must complete any activity that you are working on before logging out of the course. Otherwise, you will have to start the activity from the beginning when you return.

My progress in the course is not being tracked. What can I do?

Not logging out of the course each time you finish studying causes your progress to not be tracked. If your progress is not being tracked, click on Refresh in your internet browser while in the Unit you are currently reading.

Is there a course manual?

A PDF version of each Unit, known as the Unit eBook, is available for download to save and/or print. To access the eBook for a particular Unit, click on the Resources tab while in the Unit you wish to retrieve the eBook for and then click on Unit eBook.

How do I access the course eBooks?

Click on the Resources tab while in the Unit you wish to retrieve the eBook for and then click on Unit eBook.

What can I do if I am having difficulty viewing an eBook?

Ensure that the version of Adobe PDF Reader installed on your workstation or mobile device meets the Course System Requirements. The Course System Requirements are available on the course homepage under Course Resources. To download the latest version of Adobe PDF Reader, go to www.adobe.com/reader.

Should I read the eBooks before I start the online course?

The Unit eBooks are PDF versions of the online Units and are provided so that you may download the instructional material to save and/or print for your reference. You must complete the online course in order to obtain your Certificate of Completion.

What if I need technical support?

Ensure that your computer system meets the Course System Requirements. The Course System Requirements are available on the course homepage under Course Resources. If you still require assistance, contact the RECA Education Helpdesk. Contact information for the RECA Education Helpdesk is available by clicking on Contact RECA under the Resources tab.

What if I have a question about the instructional material?

Email the RECA Education Helpdesk at education@reca.ca. Your email must include your name, RECA ID, the Unit number or name, page number, a copy of the content in question (e.g. screenshot, copy and paste) and a description of the issue.
